📚 Booksy Boutique

Modern Book Library & Social Reading Platform

Built with React, TypeScript, Ionic & MongoDB

MVP Ready Cross-Platform

What is Booksy Boutique?

Booksy Boutique is a digital, community-based book platform that connects readers, bookstores, and libraries in a single ecosystem.

The platform combines a modern marketplace, social features, and multilingual discovery tools to simplify access to literature and strengthen local book cultures.

The EU book market is large but digitally fragmented: there is no central platform that makes community engagement, book discovery, and local retailers visible. Booksy Boutique fills this gap with a scalable technical architecture and a clear focus on cultural and social value.

Status: MVP Ready | Platform: Web, iOS & Android

✨ Key Features

Personal Library

Add, organize, and manage your entire book collection with cover uploads and ratings

Social Features

Connect with friends, share recommendations, and discover what others are reading

Cross-Platform

Mobile-first design with native iOS and Android apps using Capacitor

Rating System

Rate books and bookstores, see average ratings from the community

Shopping Cart

Add books to cart, manage purchases, and track your orders

Favorites

Save your favorite books for quick access and future reference
