What is Project Unite-7?

A cyberpunk-themed 2D platformer game featuring multiple playable characters, dynamic combat, and mobile-optimized controls.

Switch between 4 unique characters on the fly, each with their own abilities. Break walls with Rex, hack doors with Astra09, and fight through waves of intelligent AI enemies.

Built with Godot 4.4 Engine with full mobile support including virtual joystick controls, the game combines classic platformer gameplay with modern cyberpunk aesthetics and innovative mechanics.

Status: Demo in Preparation | Platform: Mobile & Desktop

🎭 Playable Characters

Switch between 4 unique heroes with special abilities

Astra09

Well-rounded character with hacking abilities. Only one who can unlock doors.

Rex

Heavy character with wall-breaking ability. Destroys obstacles with 3 hits.

Kira

Agile character with enhanced mobility and speed.

Nyxs

Mysterious character with unique combat style.

⚡ Game Features

Character Switching

Switch between 4 unique characters instantly via portrait selection system

Dynamic Combat

Fast-paced shooting mechanics with projectiles and visual feedback

Mobile Optimized

Virtual joystick controls and touch buttons for seamless mobile gameplay

Puzzle Mechanics

Wire puzzle mini-game to hack doors - connect colored wires to matching sockets

Breakable Walls

Rex can destroy obstacles with his special ability after 3 hits

Smart AI Enemies

Intelligent enemy behavior with patrol, chase, and attack patterns

  • Mouse Click: Shoot
